Frequently Asked Questions

Can I volunteer on a dig?

We offer summer volunteer programs for university students enrolled in archaeology programs.

How is Stackly funded?

Through a combination of university grants, philanthropic endowments, and museum revenue.

Are artifacts sold to private collectors?

Never. All recovered items belong to the host country or are placed in public trust museums.
