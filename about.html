Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a collective of visionary researchers, Stackly Archaeology began as a small initiative to bring modern technology into the field of antiquity. Over the years, we've grown into a global organization, leading expeditions across four continents.

Our breakthrough came in 2015 with the discovery of the Sunken City of Kerya, where our unique blend of LIDAR and marine robotics allowed us to map an entire civilization without disturbing the delicate marine ecosystem.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To uncover the truth of our past through rigorous, ethical, and technologically advanced excavation, ensuring that the legacy of ancient civilizations is preserved for future generations.

Our Vision

A world where history is accessible to all, where the artifacts of the past inform the decisions of the future, and where cultural heritage is protected unconditionally.

Research Methodology

1. Non-Invasive Surveying

Using LIDAR, ground-penetrating radar, and satellite imagery to identify sites before any soil is moved.

2. Precision Excavation

Employing micro-stratigraphy and robotic assistance to uncover delicate artifacts without damage.

3. Digital Preservation

3D scanning every find to create a permanent, accessible digital archive for researchers worldwide.
