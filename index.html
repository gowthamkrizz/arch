Civilization Timeline

3500 BCE

The dawn of early settlements in the river valleys. Evidence of rudimentary agriculture and pottery.

2100 BCE

Establishment of the first great dynasties. Monumental architecture begins to dominate the landscape.

1450 BCE

The Golden Age. Expansion of trade routes brings exotic materials like lapis lazuli and gold into the empire.

800 BCE

The mysterious collapse. Cities are abandoned, leaving behind ruins that we excavate today.
