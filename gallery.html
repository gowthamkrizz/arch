Restoration Process

1. Assessment

Every piece is microscopically examined to determine material composition and structural integrity before any cleaning begins.

2. Micro-cleaning

Centuries of dirt and calcification are painstakingly removed using laser ablation and soft-bristle micro-brushes.

3. Digital Reconstruction

Fragments are 3D scanned and reassembled digitally before physical adhesive is applied, ensuring perfect alignment.
