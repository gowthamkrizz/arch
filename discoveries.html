Featured Discovery Story

The Decoding of the Sentinel Stele

"For decades, the Sentinel Stele stood as an enigma. Found half-buried in the desert sands, its inscriptions matched no known language. In late 2025, the Stackly Epigraphy team, utilizing advanced machine learning pattern recognition alongside traditional linguistics, finally cracked the code. The stele is not a religious text, but a complex legal document outlining water rights and trade tariffs—proving that early desert societies had an intricate, functioning judicial system long before previously thought."

Prof. Marcus Thorne Head of Epigraphy
